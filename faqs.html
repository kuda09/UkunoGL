Frequently asked questions

UkinoGL questions.

What makes Ukino Global Logistics different from other companies?
We take the approach that every shipment is unique. Every shipment needs special attention and pricing that you will not get anywhere else. We earn your business with every shipment.
What type of services do you offer?
Our most popular services are international Air Freight, domestic Distribution and Delivery, and Light-Truck-Load freight. We also provide extensive Truckload, Container loads for Ocean Freight services.
Do you handle only domestic shipments?
No. Ukino Global Logistics can handle almost any type of freight shipment, to almost anywhere in the world.
Can you guarantee transit times?
The answer is YES! Transit times are guaranteed as marked on your air waybill document. Packages are tracked regularly until they are delivered safe, on time, and damage free.
When I schedule a shipment, can it be picked up on that same day?
Yes, but please allow at least a 2-hour window to allow the carrier to be dispatched to the desired location. Please be flexible within your normal business hours.
Why should I use your services if I have a current Local Carrier?
Local carriers are great for shipping locally. The problem is when they try to handle shipments out of their local delivery area, prices tend to either go up sharply or they have to hand it off to another shipping company. Either way, the customer ends up spending too much money. At Ukino, our prices are fair, very affordable, no hidden charges, no extra cost, and your merchandise is delivered as scheduled.
What if I guessed the weight of my shipment?
If you inaccurately weighed or guessed the weight of your shipment and it is reweighed during pick up or at the shipping terminal by the carrier, you will be notified and billed accordingly after immediate verification.
What type of payments do you accept?
At Ukino Global Logistics, we accept VISA, Mastercard, Discover, American Express, Bank Certified Check, Money Order, and Cash.
