Air Freight

Ukino Global Logistics offers expedited international air freight services guaranteed to meet time-definite needs for our valuable clients across the globe. When every minute counts, this is the fastest way to ship time-sensitive materials from continental US. Our global network of quality carriers and managers offer cost-effective, flexible, frequent, and reliable service.

We leverage our expertise and alliances with multiple air freight carriers to secure capacity to accommodate your air freight needs anytime. Our superior air freight services ensure that your freight is delivered safe, secure, on time, damage free, and within budget.

Sea Freight

Ukino Global Logistics specializes in the seamless movement of full container load and less-than-container load to all major international ports. We are capable of handling all types of cargo and we streamline the process with improved movement of freight and information.

Our clients benefit from our strong global network that offers frequent and flexible schedules capable of meeting their global needs. We have first class relationships with carriers that allow us to provide timely service, proper space allocation, and competitive rates for our customers.

Trucking

Ukino Global Logistics have dedicated service vehicles designed to provide transportation and distribution management solutions to all our domestic clients allowing them the ability to reduce shipping costs as well as improving their service to their customers. We offer personalized, expedited, and time-sensitive delivery solutions including same-day on-demand service, distribution, and general logistics management to give our valuable customers a competitive advantage.

Distribution

Ukino Global Logistics provides dedicated and scheduled distribution services including regularly scheduled pick-up and deliveries to multiple locations. Ukino has the capacity to successfully meet the distribution and delivery schedule of each client’s time-sensitive needs. This business strategy allows our valuable clients to focus on their core proficiencies and eliminate the need to lease, buy, or maintain a fleet of vehicles.

We are solution driven, instilling a sense of urgency in everything we do, and highly committed to safety. We work hard to deliver exceptional customer service by providing tailored solutions and watching every little detail.
