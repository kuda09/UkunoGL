Strategy and Solutions

The satisfaction of all our clients is of utmost importance in our daily business service design. Ukino Global Logistics understands the uniqueness of each client’s needs in relation to their business and the market they serve.

Ukino provides personalized solutions to meet each client’s explicit needs and designs a system that leverages time-sensitive logistics management capabilities. This strategy offers our clients the ability to focus on achieving their business goals while reducing overall shipping costs.

Ukino Global Logistics is a full service transportation and distribution management company supporting valued clients throughout the United States and across the globe. In our years of service in freight forwarding, we have built a wide clientele both domestic and international by providing superior service as seamlessly as possible in our efforts at creating and adding superior value to them. Ukino offers a wide range of logistics solutions allowing our clients to stay focused on their core proficiencies while reducing shipping costs.

Ukino Global Logistics is a service-oriented business and we understand that our clients’ needs are unique depending on their business and the market they serve. Whether by ground or air, we have the capacity to successfully meet the schedule of each client’s time-sensitive needs with our dedicated fleet of vehicles and super reliable carriers. We understand the value our clients add to our business and the direction of our business is determined by these valued clients. That is why we go above and beyond to make them successful by placing them at the center of our business.

At Ukino, we focus on exceptional customer service by continuously improving the way and manner we conduct our business and watching every little detail. Valued and quality customers, dependable carriers, a happy and healthy team is the key to our success. We treat everyone with respect and take a great deal of pride in maintaining our reputation that is based on trust, integrity, and excellence.

Our Mission

To deliver high quality service and low cost logistics solutions to our clients on time with a focus on achieving excellent customer satisfaction, creating value and competitive advantages.

Our Vision

To be one of the leaders in global logistics business while still upholding its small business service values by remaining customer-focused and delivering valuable solutions.

We are solution driven, instilling a sense of urgency in everything we do, and highly committed to safety. We work hard to deliver exceptional customer service by providing tailored solutions and watching every little detail.
